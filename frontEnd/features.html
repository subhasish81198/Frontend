Art & Craft

Our school's Art and Craft program fosters creativity and self-expression among students. In our dedicated Art Studio, students explore various mediums such as painting, drawing, sculpting, and crafting. Under the guidance of experienced art instructors, students are encouraged to unleash their imagination and develop their artistic skills. Through hands-on projects and collaborative activities, students not only learn about different art forms but also gain confidence in their ability to express themselves creatively.

Assambly

The Assembly Room serves as the heart of our school community, where students gather for important events, assemblies, and special occasions. With its spacious layout and state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment, the Assembly Room provides a welcoming space for school-wide gatherings, guest speakers, performances, and awards ceremonies. It is here that students come together to celebrate achievements, share ideas, and strengthen the bonds that unite our school.

Computer

The Computer Lab at our school is equipped with the latest technology and software, offering students a dynamic learning environment for exploring the world of computing and information technology. From coding and programming to digital design and multimedia production, students engage in hands-on activities that develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and digital literacy skills. With access to a range of educational resources and internet connectivity, the Computer Lab empowers students to harness the power of technology for learning, innovation, and creativity.

Playing Room

Our school's playing Room is a vibrant hub of activity where students engage in physical fitness, sportsmanship, and teamwork. With expansive outdoor facilities including playground equipment, sports fields, and courts, students have ample opportunities for recreation and exercise. Whether participating in organized sports leagues, informal games, or simply enjoying outdoor play, students develop essential motor skills, social skills, and a lifelong appreciation for physical activity and well-being.

Swimming Pool

Our school boasts a modern swimming pool facility that provides students with a safe and enjoyable environment to learn and practice swimming skills. Under the supervision of certified instructors, students receive expert guidance in swimming techniques, water safety, and lifesaving skills. Whether learning to swim for the first time or refining advanced strokes, our swimming program promotes physical fitness, confidence in the water, and a respect for aquatic environments.

Library

Our school library is a treasure trove of knowledge and imagination, providing students with a quiet and welcoming space for reading, research, and intellectual exploration. Stocked with a diverse collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources, the library supports students' academic pursuits and personal interests across all subject areas. With comfortable seating, study areas, and access to information technology, students are encouraged to cultivate a love of reading, develop information literacy skills, and pursue lifelong learning. Knowledgeable librarians are on hand to assist students in finding resources, conducting research, and fostering a love of literature and learning.
